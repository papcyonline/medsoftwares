## Key AI Features in Modern Pharmacy Software

### 1. Intelligent Inventory Management

AI transforms inventory from reactive to predictive:

#### Demand Forecasting
- Analyze historical sales patterns
- Account for seasonal variations
- Predict upcoming demand
- Adjust for local events

#### Automatic Reordering
- Set optimal reorder points
- Calculate economic order quantities
- Factor in supplier lead times
- Prevent stockouts automatically

#### Expiry Prevention
- Predict items at risk of expiring
- Suggest promotional pricing
- Optimize stock rotation
- Reduce waste significantly

### 2. Drug Interaction Checking

AI enhances medication safety:

#### Comprehensive Screening
- Check against patient's full medication list
- Include OTC and supplements
- Consider patient conditions
- Flag severity levels

#### Clinical Decision Support
- Provide alternative recommendations
- Explain interaction mechanisms
- Suggest monitoring parameters
- Document pharmacist review

### 3. Prescription Verification

AI assists accuracy:

#### Image Recognition
- Read handwritten prescriptions
- Verify against database
- Flag potential errors
- Speed processing

#### Dosage Validation
- Check against standard ranges
- Consider patient factors
- Alert on unusual quantities
- Suggest corrections

### 4. Customer Insights

AI improves patient care:

#### Adherence Prediction
- Identify patients at risk of non-adherence
- Suggest intervention timing
- Personalize communication
- Track improvement

#### Health Risk Assessment
- Analyze purchase patterns
- Identify potential health concerns
- Enable proactive pharmacist intervention
- Support preventive care

### 5. Workflow Automation

AI handles routine tasks:

#### Prescription Processing
- Auto-populate patient information
- Pre-verify insurance
- Queue for pharmacist review
- Streamline dispensing

#### Documentation
- Auto-generate reports
- Create compliance documentation
- Log interactions
- Reduce paperwork
