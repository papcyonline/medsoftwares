Athenahealth's athenaOne platform has gained popularity for its cloud-native approach to healthcare management. However, many practices and hospitals explore alternatives for various reasons. This comprehensive guide compares the best Athenahealth alternatives for 2026.

## Why Practices Consider Athenahealth Alternatives

- **Subscription Costs**: Ongoing fees add up significantly
- **Internet Dependency**: Cloud-only means no offline capability
- **Geographic Limitations**: US-focused platform
- **Customization Limits**: Less flexibility than some alternatives
- **Size Mismatch**: May be overkill for smaller practices
