## What Small Practices Need

### Essential Features

Unlike hospitals, small practices need streamlined solutions:

**Must-Have Features:**
- ✅ Patient scheduling
- ✅ Basic EMR/charting
- ✅ Billing and invoicing
- ✅ Patient records
- ✅ Appointment reminders
- ✅ Simple reporting

**Nice-to-Have:**
- Patient portal
- Online booking
- Telehealth
- Mobile app
- Insurance claims

### Budget Reality

Small practices typically have limited budgets:

| Practice Size | Typical Budget |
|---------------|----------------|
| Solo practitioner | $50-$300/month or under $1,000 one-time |
| 2-5 providers | $200-$800/month or under $3,000 one-time |
| 5-10 providers | $500-$1,500/month or under $5,000 one-time |
