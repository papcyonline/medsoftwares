## Argentine Healthcare Insurance

Argentina has three healthcare sectors:

### Obras Sociales (Social Insurance)
- Trade union-based insurance
- Covers employed workers
- PAMI for retirees
- Software must integrate for billing

### Prepagas (Private Insurance)
- Private health insurance
- Premium services
- Direct billing integration

### Public Sector
- Hospital público access
- SUMAR program integration
- Universal coverage

## ANMAT Compliance

Pharmacy software must support:

- Drug traceability (Trazabilidad)
- Controlled substance tracking
- Vencimiento (expiry) management
- Lote (batch) tracking
- ANMAT reporting

## AFIP Electronic Invoicing

Required for all businesses:

- Factura A, B, C tipos
- Nota de crédito/débito
- CAE/CAEA processing
- Web service integration
- Digital signature

## Currency Considerations

With peso volatility, software should:

- Support multi-currency pricing
- Allow USD reference pricing
- Handle frequent price updates
- Manage inflation adjustments
- Provide cost tracking
