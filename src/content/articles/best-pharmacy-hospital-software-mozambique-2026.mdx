Mozambique is expanding its healthcare infrastructure through the "One District, One Hospital" initiative launched in 2019. With over 30 million people and significant healthcare access challenges, the country needs modern software solutions to support this expansion. This guide explores the best [pharmacy](/solutions/pharmacy-software) and [hospital management software](/solutions/hospital-software) for Mozambique in 2026.

      Key factors shaping Mozambique's healthcare software market:

      
        - **One District, One Hospital:** National healthcare expansion initiative
        - **Portuguese Language:** Primary language for healthcare
        - **MZN Currency:** Mozambican Metical
