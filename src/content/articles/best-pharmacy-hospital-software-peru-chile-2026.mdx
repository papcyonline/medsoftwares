Peru and Chile represent two of South America's most dynamic healthcare markets. With combined populations exceeding 52 million and growing middle classes, both countries are investing heavily in healthcare technology. This guide compares the best pharmacy and hospital management software for these markets in 2026.

## Peru Healthcare Market Overview

### Market Characteristics

| Factor | Peru |
|--------|------|
| Population | 34 million |
| Healthcare spending | 5.2% of GDP |
| Pharmacy count | 25,000+ |
| Hospital count | 600+ public, 400+ private |
| Insurance coverage | SIS (45%), EsSalud (35%), Private (10%) |

### Regulatory Environment

**DIGEMID** (Dirección General de Medicamentos, Insumos y Drogas) regulates:
- Drug registration and authorization
- Pharmacy licensing
- Controlled substance tracking
- Good pharmacy practices

### Key Software Requirements for Peru

- Spanish language interface
- PEN (Peruvian Sol) currency support
- DIGEMID compliance features
- SUNAT electronic invoicing
- Integration with Yape, Plin, BCP mobile payments
- SIS/EsSalud insurance processing

## Electronic Invoicing Compliance

Both countries require electronic invoicing:

### Peru - SUNAT Requirements

- Electronic receipts (Boletas)
- Electronic invoices (Facturas)
- XML format submission
- Real-time validation
- Digital signature

### Chile - SII Requirements

- Boletas electrónicas
- Facturas electrónicas
- XML transmission
- Real-time processing
- Certificación SII

**PharmaPOS and HospitalOS** support electronic invoicing for both Peru and Chile, generating compliant documents and transmitting to tax authorities.

## Implementation Considerations

### Language and Localization

Ensure your software provides:

- Latin American Spanish (not Spain Spanish)
- Local date formats (DD/MM/YYYY)
- Local number formats (1.000,00)
- Tax identification (RUC for Peru, RUT for Chile)

### Infrastructure

Both countries have good urban connectivity but rural challenges:

- **Cities**: Reliable internet available
- **Rural areas**: Offline capability essential
- **Power**: Generally stable, UPS recommended

### Support and Training

Look for vendors offering:

- Spanish language support
- Latin American time zone coverage
- Local implementation partners
- Spanish documentation
