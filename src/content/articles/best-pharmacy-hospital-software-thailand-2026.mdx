## Thai Healthcare Insurance Systems

### Universal Coverage Scheme (UCS)

- Covers 75% of population
- NHSO managed
- Software must integrate for claims

### Social Security Scheme (SSS)

- Private sector employees
- SSO integration required

### Civil Servant Medical Benefit Scheme (CSMBS)

- Government employees
- Comptroller General integration
