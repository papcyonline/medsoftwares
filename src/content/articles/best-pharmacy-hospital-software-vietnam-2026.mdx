## BHXH Social Insurance Integration

Vietnam's Social Health Insurance (BHXH) covers over 90% of the population. Software must support:

### Required BHXH Features

1. **Patient Eligibility Check**: Verify BHXH coverage in real-time
2. **Claim Submission**: Electronic claim filing
3. **Drug Formulary**: BHXH-approved drug list management
4. **Pricing Compliance**: BHXH ceiling price enforcement
5. **Reporting**: Required BHXH reports and statistics

### Integration Methods

- **BHXH Portal API**: Direct integration with insurance system
- **XML Export**: Batch claim submission
- **HSM Token**: Secure digital signature for claims
