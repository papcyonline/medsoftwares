## Common Inventory Management Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Inaccurate Opening Balances

**Problem:** Starting with wrong counts ruins all future tracking

**Solution:**
- Conduct thorough physical count
- Verify high-value items twice
- Address discrepancies before go-live

### Mistake 2: Not Using Barcode Scanning

**Problem:** Manual entry causes errors

**Solution:**
- Invest in quality barcode scanner ($50-150)
- Ensure all products have barcodes
- Print labels for items without barcodes

### Mistake 3: Ignoring Expiry Management

**Problem:** Products expire unnoticed

**Solution:**
- Enter expiry dates during receiving
- Review expiry reports weekly
- Implement FEFO dispensing

### Mistake 4: Skipping Regular Stock Counts

**Problem:** Discrepancies grow undetected

**Solution:**
- Schedule monthly cycle counts
- Count high-value items weekly
- Investigate all variances
