Common reasons for exploring eClinicalWorks alternatives:

- **Usability Concerns**: Interface complexity
- **Support Issues**: Variable support quality
- **Implementation Problems**: Deployment challenges
- **Cost Considerations**: Seeking better value
- **Feature Gaps**: Specific needs not met

### 2. Athenahealth

Cloud-native healthcare platform.

**Strengths:**
- Modern cloud architecture
- Strong revenue cycle
- Automatic updates
- Good usability

**Limitations:**
- Subscription costs
- US-focused
- Internet dependent

### 3. NextGen Healthcare

Ambulatory-focused platform.

**Strengths:**
- Ambulatory expertise
- Specialty templates
- Good for mid-size practices
- Revenue cycle

**Limitations:**
- Complex product line
- Variable implementation
- US market focused

### 4. DrChrono

Modern cloud EHR with iPad focus.

**Strengths:**
- Modern interface
- iPad-native
- Good usability
- Reasonable pricing

**Limitations:**

- Smaller vendor
- US only

Simple cloud EHR.

**Strengths:**

- Quick setup
- Free tier available

**Limitations:**
- Limited features

- Small practice focus

Small practice management platform.
