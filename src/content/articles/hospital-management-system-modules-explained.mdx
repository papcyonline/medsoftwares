A Hospital Management System (HMS) consists of multiple integrated modules that work together to manage all aspects of hospital operations. This guide explains each module and its functions.

      ## Core Modules

      ### 1. Patient Registration

      The foundation of the HMS:

      
        - Patient demographics entry
        - Unique ID generation
        - Photo capture
        - Insurance/NHIS registration
        - Emergency contact details
        - Visit type selection
      

      ### 2. Outpatient (OPD) Module

      Manages outpatient visits:

      
        - Queue management
        - Token generation
        - Doctor consultation
        - Clinical notes
        - Prescription generation
        - Investigation orders
        - Referrals
      

      ### 3. Inpatient (IPD) Module

      Manages admitted patients:

      
        - Bed assignment
        - Ward management
        - Nursing notes
        - Treatment charts
        - Transfer management
        - Discharge summary
      

      ### 4. Emergency Module

      For emergency department:

      
        - Rapid registration
        - Triage system
        - Emergency protocols
        - Resuscitation documentation
        - Admission/discharge workflow
      

      ## Clinical Modules

      ### 5. Laboratory Module

      
        - Test ordering
        - Sample tracking
        - Result entry
        - Result validation
        - Report generation
        - Analyzer integration
      

      ### 6. Radiology Module

      
        - Imaging requests
        - Scheduling
        - Report dictation
        - Image storage (PACS)
        - Result distribution
      

      ### 7. Pharmacy Module

      
        - Drug dispensing
        - Inventory management
        - Expiry tracking
        - Drug interactions
        - Prescription verification
      

      ### 8. Operation Theatre

      
        - Surgery scheduling
        - OT availability
        - Pre-op checklist
        - Surgical notes
        - Post-op monitoring
      

      ## Support Modules

      ### 9. Billing Module

      
        - Charge capture
        - Invoice generation
        - Payment collection
        - Insurance claims
        - Receipt printing
        - Outstanding tracking
      

      ### 10. Inventory Module

      
        - Stock management
        - Purchase orders
        - Supplier management
        - Consumption tracking
        - Reorder alerts
      

      ### 11. HR & Payroll

      
        - Employee records
        - Attendance tracking
        - Leave management
        - Salary processing
        - Duty rosters
      

      ### 12. Reports & Analytics

      
        - Operational reports
        - Financial reports
        - Clinical statistics
        - Custom report builder
        - Dashboard analytics
