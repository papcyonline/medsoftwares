## What is a Patient Registration System?

A patient registration system is software that manages the process of:

- **Capturing patient demographics** - Name, contact, next of kin
- **Recording insurance information** - Coverage, policy numbers, eligibility
- **Creating medical record numbers** - Unique patient identifiers
- **Managing appointments** - Scheduling and check-in
- **Collecting consent** - Treatment and privacy agreements
