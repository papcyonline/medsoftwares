## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is Allscripts being discontinued?**
A: No. While Allscripts separated its data analytics division into Veradigm, the core EHR platforms (Sunrise and Paragon) continue active development and support.

**Q: Can HospitalOS match Allscripts' interoperability?**
A: HospitalOS supports basic HL7 integration. For hospitals requiring FHIR-based health information exchange participation, Allscripts has an advantage.

**Q: Does HospitalOS support US regulatory requirements?**
A: HospitalOS is HIPAA compliant but is not ONC-certified. US hospitals with Meaningful Use requirements should evaluate this gap.

**Q: How does Allscripts Paragon compare to HospitalOS for community hospitals?**
A: Paragon is designed for community hospitals and is Allscripts' most direct competitor to HospitalOS. Paragon offers more advanced clinical features but at 50-100x the cost.
