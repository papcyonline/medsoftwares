## The Independent Pharmacy Challenge

### What Makes Independents Different

- **Tighter budgets** - Can't afford $500+/month subscriptions
- **Smaller staff** - Often owner-operated with 2-5 employees
- **Personal relationships** - Know customers by name
- **Diverse revenue** - Front-end retail matters more
- **Local integration** - Community health partnerships

### Software Requirements

Independent pharmacies need:
- **Affordable pricing** - One-time or low monthly cost
- **Complete functionality** - Not limited "small business" versions
- **Easy to learn** - No dedicated IT staff
- **Reliable support** - Help when you need it
- **Growth capability** - Scale as you expand

## What Independent Pharmacies Actually Need

### Essential Features

Every independent pharmacy needs:

**Point of Sale**
- Fast checkout
- Barcode scanning
- Multiple payment types
- Receipt printing
- Returns processing

**Inventory Management**
- Stock tracking
- Expiry alerts
- Reorder notifications
- Supplier management
- Purchase orders

**Customer Management**
- Customer profiles
- Purchase history
- Credit accounts
- Loyalty programs
- Contact information

**Basic Reporting**
- Daily sales reports
- Inventory valuation
- Profit margins
- Top sellers
- Payment reconciliation

### Nice-to-Have Features

Depending on your market:
- Mobile money integration (Africa, Asia)
- Insurance claims processing
- Drug interaction checking
- Multi-language support
- Multi-location capability

### What You Probably Don't Need

Enterprise features that add cost without value:
- Complex workflow engines
- Advanced BI analytics
- Third-party API marketplace
- Enterprise user management
- Custom development tools
