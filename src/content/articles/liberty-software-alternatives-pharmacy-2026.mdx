## Making the Switch

### Migration Considerations

When switching from Liberty Software:

1. **Data Export**: Request complete data export
2. **Timeline**: Plan 4-6 weeks for transition
3. **Training**: Schedule staff training
4. **Parallel Running**: Brief overlap if possible
5. **Support**: Ensure vendor support during go-live

### What Data to Migrate

- Patient demographics and history
- Active prescriptions
- Insurance information
- Inventory and pricing
- Prescriber database
- Transaction history (optional)
