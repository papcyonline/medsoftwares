## What is Medical Billing Software?

Medical billing software automates the billing process, replacing manual workflows with integrated digital systems. It handles:

- **Patient data capture** - Demographics and insurance information
- **Insurance verification** - Real-time eligibility checking
- **Procedure coding** - ICD-10, CPT code management
- **Claims submission** - Electronic claim filing
- **Payment posting** - Recording payments and adjustments
- **Denial management** - Tracking and appealing rejected claims
- **Reporting** - Financial analytics and insights

## Pricing Models Explained

### Percentage of Collections
- Pay % of what you collect
- Typically 4-8%
- Aligns vendor interests
- Can be expensive for high-volume

### Per-Claim Fees
- Fixed fee per claim
- $0.25-1.00 per claim
- Predictable per transaction
- Adds up quickly

### Monthly Subscription
- Fixed monthly fee
- $100-500+ per provider
- Predictable costs
- Ongoing expense

### One-Time Purchase
- Single payment
- Own software
- No recurring costs
- Best long-term value

### Cost Comparison Over 5 Years

For a clinic processing 200 claims/month:
