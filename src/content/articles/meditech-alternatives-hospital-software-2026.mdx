MEDITECH has been a staple in community hospital IT for decades, with their Expanse platform serving over 2,300 healthcare facilities. However, hospitals increasingly explore alternatives seeking modern interfaces, better pricing, or specific capabilities. This guide compares the best MEDITECH alternatives for 2026.

## Why Hospitals Consider MEDITECH Alternatives

- **Modernization Needs**: Desire for more modern interfaces
- **Cost Optimization**: Seeking more affordable solutions
- **Implementation Speed**: Faster deployment requirements
- **Specific Features**: Needs not met by MEDITECH
- **International Expansion**: Global capability requirements
- **Size Mismatch**: Too complex for smaller facilities

## Making the Switch

For hospitals considering switching from MEDITECH:

1. **Assess Current State**: Document what's working and what's not
2. **Define Requirements**: Must-haves vs. nice-to-haves
3. **Calculate TCO**: Compare true 5-year costs
4. **Plan Data Migration**: Patient records, history, configurations
5. **Parallel Running**: Consider brief overlap period
