A Patient Management System (PMS) is the core of any healthcare facility's digital infrastructure. It manages patient information throughout their healthcare journey. This guide covers essential features every PMS should have.

      ## What is a Patient Management System?

      A PMS is software that manages:

      
        - Patient registration and demographics
        - Appointment scheduling
        - Visit tracking and history
        - Medical records
        - Communication and follow-up
        - Reporting and analytics
      

      ## Essential Features

      ### 1. Patient Registration

      
        - Quick demographic entry
        - Unique patient ID generation
        - Photo capture
        - Emergency contact details
        - Insurance/billing information
        - Duplicate detection
      

      ### 2. Appointment Scheduling

      
        - Visual calendar interface
        - Multi-provider scheduling
        - Time slot management
        - Appointment reminders (SMS)
        - Waitlist management
        - No-show tracking
      

      ### 3. Queue Management

      
        - Digital check-in
        - Token/queue number
        - Wait time display
        - Priority handling
        - Queue analytics
      

      ### 4. Medical Records

      
        - Visit history
        - Clinical notes
        - Diagnosis records
        - Prescription history
        - Lab and radiology results
        - Allergies and alerts
      

      ### 5. Communication Tools

      
        - SMS appointment reminders
        - Follow-up notifications
        - Lab result alerts
        - Bulk messaging
        - WhatsApp integration (optional)
      

      ### 6. Reporting

      
        - Patient statistics
        - Visit trends
        - No-show rates
        - Provider productivity
        - Custom reports
      

      ## Advanced Features

      
        - Patient portal access
        - Telemedicine integration
        - Mobile app for patients
        - Chronic disease management
        - Population health analytics
