Data-driven decision making separates successful pharmacies from struggling ones. Analytics and reporting tools turn your transaction data into actionable insights.

      ## Why Analytics Matter

      
        - Understand sales patterns
        - Identify top products
        - Spot slow-moving inventory
        - Track staff performance
        - Monitor profitability
      

      ## Essential Reports

      ### Sales Reports

      
        - Daily/weekly/monthly sales
        - Sales by product category
        - Sales by payment method
        - Hourly sales patterns
        - Comparative analysis
      

      ### Inventory Reports

      
        - Current stock levels
        - Stock valuation
        - Expiring products
        - Slow-moving items
        - Reorder needs
      

      ### Financial Reports

      
        - Profit margins
        - Revenue trends
        - Cost of goods sold
        - Outstanding receivables
        - Payables aging
      

      ### Customer Reports

      
        - Top customers
        - Customer purchase patterns
        - Credit balances
        - New vs returning
