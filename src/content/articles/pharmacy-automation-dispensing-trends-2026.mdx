## Key Automation Technologies

### 1. Automated Dispensing Systems

**What They Do:**
- Store and dispense medications
- Track inventory in real-time
- Verify medication selection
- Log all transactions

**Benefits:**
- Reduce dispensing errors by 90%+
- Free pharmacist time for patient care
- Improve inventory accuracy
- Enable 24/7 medication access (hospitals)

### 2. Barcode Scanning & Verification

**Applications:**
- Product lookup and pricing
- Prescription verification
- Batch/lot tracking
- Expiry date capture

**Benefits:**
- Near-zero scanning errors
- 60% faster checkout
- Complete traceability
- Regulatory compliance

### 3. AI-Powered Inventory Management

**Capabilities:**
- Demand forecasting
- Automatic reordering
- Expiry prediction
- Shortage alerts

**Results:**
- 30% reduction in waste
- 85%+ forecast accuracy
- Fewer stockouts
- Optimized cash flow

### 4. E-Prescribing Integration

**Features:**
- Direct prescriber-to-pharmacy transmission
- Formulary checking
- Prior authorization
- Refill automation

**Impact:**
- Eliminates handwriting errors
- Faster prescription processing
- Better insurance verification
- Improved patient safety
