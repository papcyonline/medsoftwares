## Essential POS Features for Pharmacies

### 1. Barcode Scanning Integration

Every pharmacy POS must support barcode scanning:

**Hardware Compatibility**:
- USB wired scanners (most reliable)
- Bluetooth wireless scanners (more flexibility)
- 1D and 2D barcode support
- Built-in camera scanning (tablet POS)

**Scanning Functions**:
- Product lookup by barcode
- Prescription verification
- Batch/lot number capture
- Expiry date encoding
- Price verification

**Speed Advantage**:
| Method | Time per Item | Errors |
|--------|--------------|--------|
| Manual entry | 15-30 seconds | 5-10% |
| Barcode scan | 1-2 seconds | Under 0.1% |

### 2. Mobile Payment Integration

Mobile payments dominate in many markets:

**Africa (Mobile Money)**:
- MTN Mobile Money (Ghana, Uganda, Rwanda, Cameroon)
- M-Pesa (Kenya, Tanzania)
- Vodafone Cash (Ghana)
- Airtel Money (Multiple countries)
- Orange Money (Francophone Africa)

**Global Mobile Wallets**:
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Samsung Pay
- WeChat Pay
- Alipay

**Health-Specific Payments**:
- FSA (Flexible Spending Account) cards
- HSA (Health Savings Account) cards
- IIAS-compliant transactions

### 3. Traditional Payment Processing

Still essential alongside mobile:
- Credit/debit cards (EMV chip + contactless)
- Cash management with drawer integration
- Split payments (multiple methods per transaction)
- Layaway and deposits

### 4. Prescription Integration

Pharmacy-specific POS must handle:
- Rx barcode scanning for verification
- Copay collection with signature capture
- Insurance claim integration
- Controlled substance documentation
- Patient consultation logging
