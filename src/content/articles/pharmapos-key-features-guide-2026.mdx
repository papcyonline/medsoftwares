PharmaPOS is a comprehensive pharmacy management system built specifically for pharmacies in Africa, Middle East, Asia, and Latin America. This guide covers every feature in detail.

![PharmaPOS Key Features](/infographics/pharmapos-features.png)

## Core Features Overview

PharmaPOS combines six essential modules into one integrated platform:

1. **Point of Sale (POS)** - Fast, accurate transactions
2. **Inventory Management** - Complete stock control
3. **Prescription Management** - Safety and compliance
4. **Mobile Money Integration** - Local payment methods
5. **NHIS/Insurance Claims** - Automated processing
6. **Offline Mode** - Works without internet

## 1. Point of Sale (POS)

### Fast Checkout

- **Barcode Scanning**: Scan products for instant lookup
- **Product Search**: Find items by name, generic name, or code
- **Keyboard Shortcuts**: Power user commands for speed
- **Quick Keys**: Frequently sold items one-click access

### Flexible Pricing

- **Multi-Unit Sales**: Sell by piece, card, or box
- **Auto Price Calculation**: Prices adjust based on unit
- **Discounts**: Percentage or fixed amount
- **Promotions**: Time-limited special pricing

### Sales Management

- **Pending Sales Queue**: Pharmacist prepares, cashier processes
- **Hold & Recall**: Save transactions for later
- **Returns Processing**: Handle returns with full tracking
- **Split Payments**: Multiple payment methods per sale

### Receipt Printing

- **Thermal Printer Support**: 58mm and 80mm printers
- **Custom Templates**: Your branding and messaging
- **Reprint Capability**: Reprint any historical receipt
- **Tax Display**: Compliant tax information

## 2. Inventory Management

### Stock Tracking

- **Real-Time Quantities**: Always know what you have
- **Multiple Locations**: Store room vs. counter stock
- **Stock Valuation**: Know your inventory value
- **Low Stock Alerts**: Never run out of essentials

### Expiry Management

- **Batch Tracking**: Track each batch separately
- **Expiry Alerts**: 30, 60, 90 day warnings
- **FEFO Enforcement**: First Expired, First Out
- **Expiry Reports**: Plan clearance sales

### Purchase Management

- **Supplier Management**: Track all your suppliers
- **Purchase Orders**: Create and send POs
- **Goods Receiving**: Receive with batch details
- **Price Comparison**: Compare supplier pricing

### Stock Operations

- **Stock Adjustments**: Document additions/removals
- **Stock Transfers**: Move between branches
- **Stock Take**: Physical count vs. system
- **Variance Reports**: Identify discrepancies

## 3. Prescription Management

### Drug Safety

- **Drug Interaction Checker**: Automatic alerts for dangerous combinations
- **Allergy Warnings**: Patient allergy cross-check
- **Dosage Guidance**: Recommended dosing information
- **Contraindications**: Condition-based warnings

### Controlled Substances

- **Schedule Tracking**: Track controlled medications
- **Register Compliance**: Maintain required records
- **Quantity Limits**: Enforce dispensing rules
- **Audit Trail**: Complete dispensing history

### Prescription Processing

- **Digital Prescriptions**: Enter prescription details
- **Refill Tracking**: Monitor refill eligibility
- **Prescription History**: Patient medication history
- **Label Printing**: Professional medication labels

## 6. Offline Mode

### How It Works

PharmaPOS stores all data locally, syncing to cloud when connected:

1. **Local Database**: Complete data on your computer
2. **Full Functionality**: All features work offline
3. **Auto-Sync**: Uploads when internet returns
4. **Conflict Resolution**: Handles data conflicts intelligently

### What Works Offline

| Feature | Online | Offline |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Sales Processing | Yes | Yes |
| Inventory Lookup | Yes | Yes |
| Customer Accounts | Yes | Yes |
| Receipt Printing | Yes | Yes |
| Mobile Money | Yes | No* |
| NHIS Claims | Yes | Queued |
| Reports | Yes | Yes |

*Mobile money requires internet; sale can be saved for later payment

### Why It Matters

In regions with unreliable internet:
- **No Lost Sales**: Continue operating during outages
- **No Downtime**: Your pharmacy never stops
- **Data Security**: Local backup protects against cloud issues
- **Cost Savings**: Don't need premium internet plans
