## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can PharmaPOS handle US e-prescribing?

PharmaPOS supports prescription management but has limited US EPCS (Electronic Prescribing for Controlled Substances) integration. For US pharmacies requiring full e-prescribing, PioneerRx is stronger.

### Does PioneerRx work offline?

No, PioneerRx requires internet connectivity. Outages can disrupt operations. PharmaPOS works fully offline.

### Which has better support?

Both offer good support. PharmaPOS provides multi-language support globally. PioneerRx focuses on US English support with extensive documentation.

### Can I switch between them?

Yes, data can be migrated between systems. PharmaPOS makes migration straightforward with import tools.
