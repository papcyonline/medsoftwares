## What is Revenue Cycle Management?

Revenue Cycle Management encompasses all administrative and clinical functions that contribute to capturing, managing, and collecting patient service revenue. It spans from the moment a patient schedules an appointment to the final payment collection.

### The Revenue Cycle Stages:

1. **Pre-Registration** - Patient scheduling and insurance verification
2. **Registration** - Demographics and insurance capture
3. **Charge Capture** - Recording services provided
4. **Coding** - Translating services to billable codes
5. **Claim Submission** - Sending claims to payers
6. **Payment Posting** - Recording received payments
7. **Denial Management** - Handling rejected claims
8. **Patient Collections** - Collecting patient responsibility
