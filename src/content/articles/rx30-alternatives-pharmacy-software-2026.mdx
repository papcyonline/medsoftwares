## Key Considerations When Switching from Rx30

### Data Migration

The most critical concern when switching pharmacy systems is data migration:

- **Patient Records**: Ensure complete demographic and history transfer
- **Prescription Data**: Active Rx and refill history
- **Insurance Information**: Payer data and plan details
- **Inventory**: Current stock levels and pricing

Most vendors offer migration assistance, but verify what's included.

### Training Requirements

Consider the training investment for each alternative:

- **PharmaPOS**: 2-4 hours basic training
- **PioneerRx**: 1-2 weeks comprehensive training
- **PrimeRx**: 1-2 weeks+ depending on customization
- **BestRx**: 4-8 hours training

### Workflow Disruption

Plan for a transition period:

1. Choose a slower period for conversion
2. Run parallel systems briefly if possible
3. Have vendor support available during go-live
4. Train all staff before the switch
