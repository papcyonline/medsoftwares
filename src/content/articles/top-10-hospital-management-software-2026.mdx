**HospitalOS** is our top recommendation for small hospitals (under 200 beds) due to its affordable one-time pricing, fast implementation, and comprehensive features without enterprise complexity.

### How long does implementation take?

| Solution Type | Timeline |
|---------------|----------|
| Cloud SaaS | 1-3 months |
| HospitalOS | 2-4 weeks |
| Mid-market | 6-12 months |
| Enterprise | 12-36 months |

### Cloud vs. on-premise: which is better?

**Choose Cloud if:**
- You want faster implementation
- You have reliable internet
- You prefer predictable monthly costs
- You want automatic updates

**Choose On-Premise/Hybrid (like HospitalOS) if:**
- Internet is unreliable
- You want to avoid ongoing fees
- Data sovereignty matters
- You're in a developing market

### What are the must-have features?

Essential hospital software features:
1. Patient registration and scheduling
2. Electronic medical records
3. Billing and revenue cycle
4. Pharmacy management
5. Laboratory integration
6. Reporting and analytics
