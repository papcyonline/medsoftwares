## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best pharmacy software for small pharmacies?

For small pharmacies, we recommend **PharmaPOS** for its one-time pricing and comprehensive features. If you're US-based and prefer subscription, **BestRx** offers good value.

### How much does pharmacy management software cost?

Costs vary widely:
- **One-time licenses**: $499-$25,000
- **Monthly subscriptions**: $100-$1,000+/month
- **Enterprise solutions**: $10,000-$100,000+/year

### Can I switch pharmacy software easily?

Switching requires planning but is manageable. Most vendors offer data migration assistance. Expect 2-4 weeks for transition.

### Do I need cloud or on-premise software?

- **Cloud**: Better for multiple locations, remote access
- **On-premise**: Better for areas with poor internet, data control
- **Hybrid** (like PharmaPOS): Best of both worlds

### What features are essential for pharmacy software?

Must-have features:
1. Prescription management
2. Inventory tracking
3. Point of sale
4. Reporting
5. Insurance/claims processing
